How to make rice

Description

Rice, a tiny, versatile grain, is a staple food for over half the world. Its journey from seed to fluffy dish involves cultivation in warm climates. It comes in various forms, from long and slender to short and sticky, each offering a unique texture and flavor. Simple to cook, rice is a blank canvas for countless culinary creations, enjoyed as a side dish, main course, or even dessert.

Ingredients

Ingredients required to make bowl of rice.

  • Rice 1cup
  • Water 1.5cups
  • Cooking pot or rice cooker
  • Seasonings (optional)

Steps

You should follow these steps to cook rice.

  1. Rinse the rice
  2. Combine rice with water
  3. Put them if cooking pot or rice cooker
  4. Turn on the heat
  5. If you have rice cooker, put rice and water in it and adjust time to 7 - 10 minutes according to temperature
  6. Your fluffy rice is ready.
