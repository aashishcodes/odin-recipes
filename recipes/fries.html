How to make fries at home?

Description

French fries, those golden-hued, crispy delights, are a beloved side dish or snack. Made from potatoes cut into various shapes,most commonly thin strips, they are deep-fried until golden brown and often sprinkled with salt. Their versatility shines through their ability to be enjoyed plain, dipped in ketchup or other sauces, or even topped with more substantial ingredients like cheese and gravy. Whether enjoyed hot and crispy or soft and fluffy, fries remain a universally adored comfort food.

  5. Rinse and Dry

  7. First Fry
  8. Second Fry
  9. Your fries are ready
