How to make Burger


Description

The humble burger, a culinary icon, transcends continents and cultures. Its core elements are simple: a juicy patty nestled between soft buns. Yet, its versatility is boundless. From classic cheeseburgers to gourmet creations with exotic toppings, the possibilities are endless. Burgers offer a satisfying and convenient meal, enjoyed on the go or savored at a leisurely pace. Whether dressed with classic ketchup and mustard or adorned with gourmet sauces and exotic ingredients, the burger remains a timeless symbol of comfort and deliciousness.

Ingredients

Following are the ingredients required to make a burger

  • Ground chicken (around 1/2lb per patty)
  • Breadcrumbs (1/4 cup)
  • Egg (1)
  • Onion (finely chopped, optional)
  • Garlic (miced, optional)
  • Seasonings: salt, pepper, paprika, other disired spices

Steps

Following is the step by step process to make a chicken burger:

  1. First you need to gather the ingredients mentioned above.
  2. Mix the patty
  3. Shape the patties
  4. Cook the patty
  5. Toast the buns
  6. Assemble the burger
  7. Add toppings.
  8. Enjoy your burger.
